Programming Assignment 2

Activewear Brands sold in same online retailer

[move mouse over graph]

Explanation

This dataset shows the relationship between activewear brands and online retailers.

  • Each node represents one brand
  • Brands are linked together if they are sold in the same retailer.
  • Node size represents the number of retailers that sell that particular brand
  • Brands with the same color are sold by the same retailer(s)

Interesting observations

  • There are a couple of clusters of brands that are sold by only one retailer (Sears and JCPenny)
  • A few brands are sold by many retailers (Nike, Under Armour, Adidas)
  • Many brands are sold only by one retailer, usually the owner of the brand
